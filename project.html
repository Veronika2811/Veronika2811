Random-quotes

Random-quotes app. Created during the learning process at RSSchool.

  • When loading, reloading the page, when you click on the application button, a random quote is displayed.
  • You can choose one of two quote display languages: en/ru.
  • The change of the quote is accompanied by a change in the background image of the page, a clock and a music player are added.

Tic-tac-toe

Tic-tac-toe game in custom design.

  • At the end of the game, its result is displayed - the winning piece and the number of moves from the beginning of the game to its completion.
  • The results of the last 10 games are stored in local storage.
  • There is a table of records, which displays the results of the previous 10 games.
  • Sound when you click on the playing field, change the theme.
